Safety FAQs

How are drivers vetted on RideShare Ghana?

All drivers must submit a valid driver's license, vehicle registration, and insurance documents. We verify these with the appropriate authorities and conduct background checks before approving any driver.

What should I do if I feel unsafe during a ride?

If you feel unsafe, ask the driver to stop at the nearest public place and exit the vehicle. Immediately contact our 24/7 safety line (+233 24 123 4567) to report the incident and request assistance.

How does RideShare Ghana handle cash payments safely?

We recommend agreeing on the payment amount before the trip begins. Count money discreetly and avoid displaying large sums. Drivers should keep earnings secured and out of sight during trips.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We encourage drivers to regularly sanitize their vehicles and provide hand sanitizer. Both drivers and passengers may request mask-wearing during trips. Vehicles should be well-ventilated when possible.

Can I request a driver of a specific gender?

While we don't currently offer gender-based matching, you can view driver profiles before booking and choose based on ratings and reviews that make you feel most comfortable.

What happens if a driver cancels last minute?

Frequent cancellations negatively impact a driver's rating and may lead to suspension from the platform. If your driver cancels, we'll notify you immediately and help you find an alternative ride.
